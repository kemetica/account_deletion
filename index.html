Account Deletion Process for Kemetica

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your Kemetica account:

  1. Step 1: Click on your avatar icon in the top app bar.
  2. Step 2: You will be directed to your profile screen.
  3. Step 3: In the top right corner, you will find a settings icon. Click on it.
  4. Step 4: After clicking the settings icon, you will enter the settings screen.
  5. Step 5: Click on the Profile Settings option.
  6. Step 6: At the bottom of the Profile Settings screen, you will find an option to delete your account permanently.
